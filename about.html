About Me

Hi, I'm Jasnoor! I'm a Front-End Developer with a passion for creating intuitive, modern websites that are both visually appealing and highly functional. I focus on building fast, accessible, and user-friendly websites that deliver great experiences across all devices.

What Got Me Into Coding

After high school, I first got into Information Technology but quickly found it wasn’t the right fit. I realized I enjoyed the challenge of building things, and that curiosity led me to web development. I joined BCIT’s Front-End Web Developer program — an incredible journey that helped me grow as both a designer and developer.

What I'm Passionate About

I'm passionate about crafting websites that feel intuitive, fast, and modern. I focus on accessibility, clean interfaces, and smooth performance, ensuring every site feels natural to use while maintaining technical precision.

Beyond Coding

Outside of development, I love spending time with friends, exploring new restaurants, and playing sports or video games — it’s a great balance between creativity, teamwork, and fun.
